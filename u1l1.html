🧠 What is Intelligence?

Intelligence is the ability to learn from experience, reason logically, solve problems, make decisions, communicate effectively, and adapt to changing situations.

Characteristics of Intelligence

  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Problem Solving
  • Adaptability
  • Communication
  • Creativity

🤖 What is Artificial Intelligence?

Definition:
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that develops systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, language understanding, perception, and decision-making.

Simple Definition

AI enables machines to think, learn, and make decisions in ways that resemble human intelligence.

Examples Around Us

  • ChatGPT
  • Google Search
  • Google Maps
  • YouTube Recommendations
  • Netflix Recommendations
  • Alexa & Siri
  • Face Recognition
  • Email Spam Filters

⚡ Traditional Computing vs AI Systems

Parameter Traditional Computing AI Systems
Approach Rule Based Data Driven
Logic Programmed Explicitly Learns from Data
Input Type Structured Structured + Unstructured
Unknown Situations Fails Predicts Best Outcome
Example Calculator ChatGPT

The Core Difference

  • Traditional Computing: Data + Rules → Output
  • AI Systems: Data + Output → Learns Rules

📈 Why is AI Important?

  • Automates repetitive tasks
  • Improves productivity
  • Reduces human errors
  • Supports decision-making
  • Creates innovative products
  • Enhances customer experiences

🕒 Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

1950 – Alan Turing proposed the Turing Test.
1956 – John McCarthy coined the term AI.
1960–1980 – Expert Systems Era.
1997 – IBM Deep Blue defeated Garry Kasparov.
2010s – Deep Learning Revolution.
2020s – Generative AI and LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini).

🎯 Types of Artificial Intelligence

1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)

Designed to perform specific tasks efficiently.

  • ChatGPT
  • Google Maps
  • Recommendation Systems

2. General AI (Strong AI)

Human-level intelligence across all domains. Currently does not exist.

3. Super AI

Hypothetical AI that surpasses human intelligence in every aspect.
