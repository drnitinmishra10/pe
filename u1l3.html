📖 Introduction to Generative AI

Definition:
Generative AI is a category of Artificial Intelligence that creates new content such as text, images, audio, video, code, and designs[cite: 1].

Unlike traditional AI, which analyzes existing data, Generative AI produces entirely new content based on patterns learned from huge datasets[cite: 1].

⚡ Traditional AI vs Generative AI

Traditional AI Generative AI[cite: 1]
Analyzes Data Creates Data
Classifies Objects Generates Objects
Predicts Outcomes Creates New Outputs
Detects Fraud Writes Reports
Recognizes Images Generates Images[cite: 1]

🧠 How Generative AI Works

Large Dataset → Training → AI Model → Prompt → Generated Output

Step 1: Training

AI models are trained using billions of examples from books, websites, images, videos, and other sources.

Step 2: Pattern Learning

The model learns relationships, patterns, and structures in data.

Step 3: Prompt Processing

A user provides instructions known as a Prompt[cite: 1].

Step 4: Content Generation

The AI generates content based on the prompt and learned patterns[cite: 1].

💬 Understanding Prompts

Example 1: Weak Prompt

Write about AI.

Example 2: Better Prompt

Act as a professor. Explain Artificial Intelligence in 150 words with examples for first-year engineering students[cite: 1].

Better prompts generate better outputs[cite: 1]. This concept forms the foundation of Prompt Engineering[cite: 1].
